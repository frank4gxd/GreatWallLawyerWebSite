Successful Results

Selected litigation highlights.

2025

The River Trading Limited v CLH Construction Limited [2025] NZHC 2326

Acted for a creditor in a successful application to liquidate a debtor company.

2024

LG Home Trading Limited v Bridgeman Concrete Limited [2024] NZHC 1418

Acted for a company in a successful application to set aside a $1.1 million statutory demand from the company's supplier.

2023

Tian v Xu [2023] NZHC 3259, NZHC 2443, NZHC 1261

Acted for a plaintiff in a successful claim to enforce Chinese judgments in New Zealand for RMB 2.3 million plus interest, and obtained freezing orders.

2022

Tao v Malone [2022] NZHC 1657

Acted for a widow in a successful interim injunction restraining distribution of $1.8 million from her deceased husband's estate.
